A Day in the Life of a Byte Camper

  1. 9:00 am

  2. 10:00 am

  3. 11:00 am

  4. 12:00 pm

  5. 1:00 pm

  6. 2:00 pm

  7. 3:00 pm

9:00 am - Arrival

Camp is now in session! The campers are in the room and the instructors are going over the plan for the day.

We'll do some work on our projects for about an hour while the leaders interject with lessons and tips!

10:00 am - Break

It's time for a break! Let's head outside for some games! Camoflauge, dodgeball, ninja, who knows?

All outdoor breaks are minimum 45 minutes and happen rain or shine - make sure to check the weather!

11:00 am - Creating

We're back inside (after a much needed water break) for some coding, animating, whatever! We've got more to teach, and more to learn.

12:00 pm - Lunch

It's time to #lunchitup! Campers head outside with the lunches they brought for some refueling and some more games - Werewolf and "I'm Going Camping" are always popular!

1:00 pm - Working

Now full and exhausted from running in the sun, we'll head back in for another hour of learning, working, and creating!
