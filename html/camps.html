Make your own clay characters come to life, just like Shaun the Sheep! Participants will work with a partner to build their own clay characters, sets and props, develop their own script, shoot their movie scene, and then learn to edit and add sounds and special effects to complete the whole project. Don't worry parents, there will be plenty of time devoted to outdoor games and activities!
Ages: 9 - 12 yrs old
Cost: $200.00 - $275.00
Length: 4/5 Days
