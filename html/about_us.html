About Us

What is Byte Camp?

Byte Camp Education Society is a non-profit organization that has been providing engaging, affordable creative technology programs for kids since 2003. We have active partnerships with over 100 schools and community centres in Metro Vancouver, Victoria, Nanaimo, Calgary, and the Okanagan. Over 20,000 kids have come through our programs learning animation, web design, programming, video editing, 3D modelling and more!
